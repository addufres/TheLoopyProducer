Success Quote of the Day - March 1, 2017

"Try not to become a man of success, but rather try to become a man of value." - Albert Einstein
[caption id="attachment_376" align="alignright" width="377"] Credit: Brainy Quote[/caption] No introduction is needed for one of the greatest minds in American history. Albert Einstein understood and applied this principle in his life's work. Think back, to when he was struggling in school as a young child. Yet, even at this age he had grand visions of what his value could be. His path to success was not to focus on success. Rather, it was to forge his path through following his vision and dreams for himself. This meant bringing value to the world, through his scientific and technological advancements.

What is "becoming a man of value"?

In my life, I have held the belief that if you create something that people want then the money and success will follow naturally. I mean come on, this whole website is devoted to helping others succeed. I want to have the peace of mind at the end of the day that I have helped somebody. If I can help one person then I am a success. The more value I create with what I bring to the table, the more success I will reach. The Value that is brought to the table can come in many forms. To some of you it may mean being a present and loving parent. Or, teaching your kid to be the best version of themselves through example. To some, it could mean finally going ahead and starting that business. Maybe, beginning that blog or advice column. Value can come in many forms but the important part is living your life through those values. Rather than, living through the vain outlook of wanting appreciation or "success".
